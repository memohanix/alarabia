Message From Chairman

Its a pleasure to express my view on the future prospective of Nepali job seekers in the world. In the recent times, especially in Middle East and Gulf countries, there is a major demand for honest, sincere and hard working workforces as well as skilled personnel and professionals.

Thus, we are supplying manpower in accordance with sponsor's need such as good looking, strong, energetic and capable of doing their job in an adverse weather conditions in Middle East Countries.The main reason for this preference for a Nepali workforce are, they are hardworking, honest and sincere in their dealings both with employer and the host nations, do not indulge in politics of the region and by large do not engage themselves in activities that are detrimental to the interest of the host nations as well as Nepal.
