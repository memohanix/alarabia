About Us

It gives us immense pleasure to introduce ourselves - AL ARABIA OVERSEAS PVT. LTD. as a pioneer overseas employment agency in Nepal managed by a dynamic team of professional, efficient and experienced personnel.

Approved by Nepal Government - Department of Labor, License No. 744/064/065, we expertise in the supply of unskilled, semi-skilled, skilled and highly skilled professional work foce in all countries.We keep our valued customers first in all aspects and ensure that the workers are imparted with "on the job" refresher training at a recognized Polytechnic institution before they join their job overseas.

The selection of able/deserving candidates plays key role on customer satisfaction and ultimate success. Our expertise in the field of man power management, particularly selection, requirement, training and supervision for the last 15 years has proved our services outstanding, economical and time saving to our clients.

Al ARABIA OVERSEAS PVT. LTD. Nepal is committed to excel as human resources suppliers in the job market and our excellent track record so far, is witness to it. Our well maintained data bank of various categories of work force enables us to meet the requirements of our valued customers. In brief: "We deliver what we commit - that's why we are different from others". Now we look forward to getting the opportunity and honor of fulfilling your demand.
